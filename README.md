# 🎙️ Khak AI Voice Studio

**Khak AI Voice Studio** is a powerful, free, and unlimited AI Text-to-Speech (TTS) tool that transforms your written text into high-quality, natural human voices. Designed specifically for Content Creators, YouTubers, and Educators worldwide.

## 🚀 Live Demo
Experience the app live here:
👉 [khak-ai-voice-studio.streamlit.app](https://khak-ai-voice-studio.streamlit.app/)

## ✨ Key Features

* **Unlimited Usage:** No character limits. Generate long-form audio for free without any hidden costs.
* **100+ Global Languages:** Full support for English 🇺🇸, Urdu 🇵🇰, Hindi 🇮🇳, Arabic 🇸🇦, Spanish 🇪🇸, French 🇫🇷, and many more.
* **Natural AI Characters:** Choose from a wide variety of professional male and female voices that sound remarkably human.
* **Advanced Customization:** Fine-tune the **Pitch** and **Speed** to match the perfect tone for your project.
* **High-Quality MP3 Downloads:** Instant audio generation with easy download options for your videos or presentations.
* **No Registration Required:** Start creating immediately—no account or login needed.

## 📖 How to Use
1.  **Select Language:** Choose your desired language from the "Select Country/Language" menu.
2.  **Pick a Character:** Select a male or female AI voice from the available list.
3.  **Enter Script:** Paste your text into the script box.
4.  **Generate:** Click the "Generate Voice" button and download your audio in seconds.
